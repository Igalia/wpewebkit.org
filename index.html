WebKit port optimized for embedded devices

WPE WebKit allows embedders to create simple and performant systems based on Web platform technologies. It is designed with flexibility and hardware acceleration in mind, leveraging common 3D graphics APIs for best performance.

Learn about WPE Architecture

WebKit powers billions of embedded devices.

Embedded "browsers" are more than just a way that people can browse the Web as we commonly think about them: They power increasingly many of the user interfaces that you encounter every day. From cable boxes to automobile and airplane infotainment systems, to kiosks, digital signage, smart appliances and video game consoles - embdedded browsers are everywhere.

No matter your backgroud, we'd love to help you learn more about WPE WebKit.
