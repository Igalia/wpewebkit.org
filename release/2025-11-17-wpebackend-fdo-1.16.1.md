This is the first bug fix release in the stable 1.16 series.

### What's new in WPEBackend-fdo 1.16.1?

- Support newer versions of Mesa where format/modifier events have been
  deprecated by implementing the needed parts of the DMA-BUF feedback
  protocol in the nested compositor.
